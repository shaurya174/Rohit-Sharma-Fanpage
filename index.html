Rohit Sharma (born 30 April 1987) is an Indian international cricketer and the captain of Indian cricket team in Test and ODI formats. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest ODI opening batters of all time.He is a right-handed batsman who plays for Mumbai Indians in Indian Premier League and for Mumbai in domestic cricket. Rohit previously captained India in all three formats. After leading India to the victory at the 2024 Men's T20 World Cup, he announced his retirement from T20Is.He also led India to victory in the 2025 ICC Champions Trophy.
